Rules of Snake

The player controls a snake with red head and yellow tail.
The snake wants to eat the green apples the spawn on the board from time to time.
Each apple earns you a full point! The snake eats by running into the apples with its head; but if it runs into its own body it's GAME OVER!

Game option

Normal: after eating an apple, the speed of snake increase!
Relax: the speed of the game is constant.
